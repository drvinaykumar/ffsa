Freedom, Foolishness, Stupidity, and Arrogance

A comprehensive 25-lecture course exploring the philosophical, psychological, sociological, and historical dimensions of four interconnected human conditions.

Explore the Course

Course Themes

Freedom

Explore philosophical conceptions of freedom, its historical development, and contemporary manifestations.

Foolishness

Understand the psychology of foolishness, cognitive biases, and their impact on decision-making.

Stupidity

Analyze systemic and individual factors that promote stupidity and its distinction from ignorance.

Arrogance

Examine the relationship between arrogance, power, and self-deception in individuals and institutions.
